## 1. PURPOSE

This policy outlines the procedures for requesting and managing annual leave, unpaid leave, and related matters for all employees of TechCorp Ltd.

## 2. ANNUAL LEAVE ENTITLEMENT

### 2.1. Standard Allocation

All full-time employees are entitled to **25 working days** of paid annual leave per calendar year, in addition to public holidays. Part-time employees receive a pro-rata allocation based on their contracted hours.

**Enhanced Entitlement:**

- Employees with 5+ years of service: **28 working days**
- Senior Management positions: **30 working days**
- Leave entitlement accrues from the first day of employment

### 2.2. Public Holidays

In addition to annual leave, employees are entitled to **8 public holidays** per year as designated by the company calendar.

## 3. REQUESTING ANNUAL LEAVE

### 3.1. Submission Process

To request annual leave, employees must:

1. Submit a request through **BambooHR** (accessible at bamboohr.techcorp.com) **at least 15 working days** in advance for leaves of 5+ days
2. Submit requests **at least 5 working days** in advance for shorter periods (1-4 days)
3. Obtain approval from their line manager
4. Receive confirmation from the HR Department

**Important:** Requests submitted with less notice will be considered on a case-by-case basis and may be declined due to business requirements.

### 3.2. Peak Period Restrictions

During peak business periods (typically Q4), leave requests may be subject to additional approval requirements to ensure adequate staffing levels.

## 4. CARRYING LEAVE FORWARD

### 4.1. Annual Leave Rollover

Employees may carry forward unused annual leave to the next calendar year under the following conditions:

- Maximum **5 working days** may be carried forward
- Carried-over days must be used by **March 31st** of the following year
- Rollover must be approved by line manager and submitted via BambooHR

**Note:** Any unused leave exceeding 5 days will be **forfeited** at year-end. Employees are encouraged to take their full entitlement within the calendar year.

## 5. SICKNESS DURING ANNUAL LEAVE

If an employee falls ill during scheduled annual leave:

1. **Notify** your line manager and HR Department on the first day of illness
2. Obtain a **medical certificate** (fit note) from a registered medical practitioner for absences of 7+ days
3. Submit medical documentation to HR within **3 working days** of return to work
4. Days covered by valid medical certification will be **recredited** to your annual leave balance and recorded as sick leave instead

Medical certificates must clearly state the period of illness and reason for inability to work.

## 6. UNPAID LEAVE

### 6.1. Requesting Unpaid Leave

Employees may request unpaid leave for personal circumstances when annual leave is exhausted or for extended absences.

**Application Process:**

1. Submit a written request through BambooHR **at least 20 working days** in advance
2. Provide detailed reason and requested dates
3. Obtain approval from line manager and HR Director

Unpaid leave requests are considered based on business needs and individual circumstances. Approval is not guaranteed.

### 6.2. Special Circumstances

In exceptional circumstances (bereavement, family emergency, parental leave), statutory rights apply. Please contact HR immediately for guidance.
